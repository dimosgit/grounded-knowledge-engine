# Blue Envelope Hold

The team described this informally as the blue envelope pause: seal the cobalt
packet, stop before the courier handoff, and wait for the second signature.
People may vaguely recall it as the blue packet waiting step or the envelope
pause before handoff.

The durable rule is to obtain a second signature before releasing the packet
to the courier.
